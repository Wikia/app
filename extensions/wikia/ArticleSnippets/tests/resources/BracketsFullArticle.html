Once TUPA came to the planet seamonster left the server(for unknown reasons), giving the mayor-ship of the town to SlimeDungeon. The town lasted for a little longer until he renamed it to Ghosttown, Then Darth_Octo joined the town, so Slime could give him mayor-ship. Darth renamed the town Cordelia from a random space name generator.
